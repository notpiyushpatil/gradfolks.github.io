OUR PROCESS TO GET YOU IN THE LAND OF YOUR DREAMS

  • Step 1: Book our calenders unbashed

STEP 1: BOOK OUR CALENDARS UNABASHED

“Embark on a journey of self-discovery and personal growth with our exclusive program, tailored to a select few each year. Our goal is to offer a unique and individualized experience, and we're committed to working with only a select number of participants annually. The initial phase includes exploratory conversations and a phone screening to ensure our program aligns with your needs.
As a gesture of our commitment, we are also offering a complimentary 30-minute counselling session to anyone interested in gaining further insight into Stage 1 of our program.”

STEP 2: BUILDING PILLARS OF YOUR FUTURE

"Unlock the potential of your aspirations with our collaborative process of goal-setting and strategy development. Together, we will craft a personalized roadmap to success through a unique process that values the active participation of all stakeholders. Join us in this exciting stage as we work closely with both students and parents to achieve greatness."

STEP 3: REIMAGINING YOUR CHRONICLES

“From identifying your passions and motivations to uncovering the magical moments in your life, this stage is dedicated to crafting the narrative that makes you, you. Leave a lasting impression on the admissions committee by presenting your unique story, crafted by our expert guidance. We understand what colleges are looking for in a candidate and together we will work to uncover the defining moments of your life and structure them in a way that will showcase your strengths, passions, and what makes you truly stand out. Allow us to take the reins and write a story that will set you apart from the rest and secure your spot in the college of your choice."

STEP 4: REVELATION OF YOUR NEW HOME

"Discover your dream school and explore new possibilities with our tailored college selection service. Whether you have a school in mind or are open to new possibilities, we will create a shortlist of top-fit colleges. Based on your academic interests and goals, we will help you with a curated list of institutions that align with your aspirations. From identifying top-choice schools to assessing college readiness, we will guide you every step of the way to secure your spot in the perfect institution for you. Together we will assess your college readiness, determine the best-fit schools to apply to and strategize backup options. Take the first step towards your future and let us help you find the college of your dreams."

STEP 5: CONNECTING ALL YOUR DOTS

“From structuring your writing to meeting university-style guidelines, we'll provide you with the tools and support you need to create a standout application. Our team will work closely with you throughout the drafting process, providing feedback and guidance to ensure your application shines. We will work together to structure your thoughts and provide templates that adhere to the university’s vision. Step on this stage and let's craft a polished, compelling narrative that brings your story to life, leaving a lasting impression on the admissions committee.”

STEP 6: CUSHIONING YOUR FINANCES

“We understand that finances can be a significant factor in the college application process, which is why we're here to help you identify and apply for internal and external scholarships that align with your interests and needs. From crafting an effective scholarship application to navigating the application process, we'll be by your side every step of the way to ensure you have the best chance of securing the funding you need.

Take control of your finances and make your college dream a reality with expert guidance on scholarship applications.”

STEP 7: PULL OUT ALL THE STOPS

“The final stretch is here, and it's time to pull everything together and make your application shine. In this stage, we bring all the pieces of the puzzle together to create a polished and compelling version of your application, which involves coordinating with all stakeholders such as your school, professors, and undergraduate institutions to gather all the necessary elements such as transcripts, letters of recommendation and resumes, and package them in a way that highlights your strengths and make sure you've presented the best version of yourself to the admissions committee.”

STEP 8: DISCOVER YOUR PERFECT FIT

“Don't leave your college decision to chance! With all your offers in hand, it's time to take action and choose the college that will give you the best overall experience. We'll guide you through evaluating the pros and cons of each college, considering financials and determining which one aligns with your goals and aspirations. Take the first step to make the ultimate decision on where to attend college and contact us today to start your journey towards the perfect college fit.”

You're going to be on your way to studying at one of the most prestigious institutions in the world. We can't wait to help you kickstart your international education journey!
